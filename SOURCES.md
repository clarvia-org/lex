# Sources and Rights

## Policy

- Official-source-only policy: We only publish legislation from official publishers.
- Apache-2.0 boundary: Software, schemas, and docs are Apache-2.0.
- Source preservation: Legislation remains subject to its source-specific rights.
- Attribution rule: We must attribute the official publisher exactly as requested.
- Prohibition: No unresolved rights. If redistribution rights are unclear, the law cannot merge.

## Rights Review Procedure

Before a country's first law can be added, the contributor must verify open data redistribution rights, document the license, and populate `source.yml`.
