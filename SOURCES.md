# Sources and Rights

## Policy

- **Official-source-only:** lex only publishes legislation from official publishers.
- **Apache-2.0 boundary:** Software, schemas, and project documentation are Apache-2.0. Legislation is not relicensed.
- **Source preserved:** The exact official file is retained beside every normalized Markdown.
- **Attribution required:** The official publisher must be attributed exactly as their terms require.
- **Unresolved rights prohibited:** If redistribution rights are unclear, the law cannot merge.

## Rights Review Procedure

Before a country's first law can be added, the contributor must:

1. Identify the official publisher and their open-data or redistribution terms.
2. Verify that the terms permit retention and redistribution.
3. Document the licence, attribution rule, and terms URL in `countries/<cc>/source.yml`.
4. Record the review date in `rights_reviewed_at`.

If the terms are ambiguous, open an issue and do not merge until a maintainer resolves it.
